Strawberry Cake

Ingredients

15.25 oz. Betty Crocker Strawberry Cake Mix 1 box
1 cup Water
½ cup Chobani Vanilla Greek Yogurt
3 eggs
8 oz. Cool Whip {or Cool Whip Lite} for topping
Optional: 12 Fresh Strawberries for topping

Instructions

Mix together Cake Mix, Water, Yogurt, and Eggs until well combined.
Spray 9×13 Nonstick Cake Pan with Pam Cooking Spray, and pour batter into it.
Bake at 350 degrees for approx. 30 minutes, or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
Let cake cool completely, then ‘frost’ with a thick layer of Cool Whip.
Top each piece with a fresh strawberry and ENJOY!
