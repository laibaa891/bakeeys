About Us!

Welcome To bakeeys

Established in 1998, BAKEEYS revolutionized the concept of the bakery chain in Karachi by introducing a culture of innovation in product development and providing easy access with branches in every neighbourhood across the city.
Over the last 25 years, we've expanded to become the largest bakery retail network with 26 outlets in the city and we're not done yet - in fact, we're growing every day!
Our mission is to give our customers the most authentic and artisanal bakery experience at the best price.
All our products - whether it's bread, biscuits, sandwiches, cakes or brownies - are freshly baked every day using the best natural ingredients and time-honoured processes.
We know our customers are deeply concerned about what goes into their food so we work hard to ensure that every batch is baked from scratch and is preservative and additive-free as far as possible.
Come on over and experience how we're baking it better.
